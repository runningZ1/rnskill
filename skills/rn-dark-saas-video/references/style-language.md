# Style Language

## Visual World

This style is a "dark UI magic stage": a product interface appears to be
summoned from a black spatial void. The product should feel capable, local,
open, fast, and slightly cinematic.

## Palette

- background: near black, not blue slate
- particle grain: low-opacity gray-white specks
- horizon glow: deep purple at the bottom edge, soft and weak
- foreground text: clean white
- secondary text: muted gray
- action gradient: cyan -> electric blue -> magenta
- surfaces: charcoal panels with thin gray borders

Avoid beige, warm orange, heavy purple-blue gradient backgrounds, and persistent
horizontal neon line patterns.

## Typography

Use large, confident sans-serif display text. The style depends on scale:

- opening/ending hero text: very large, usually centered or slightly left
- UI labels: small but crisp
- buttons: medium weight, rounded, glowing
- no decorative serif, no playful display font

Use kinetic text states: typing, word replacement, smear, horizontal blur,
split-word spacing, and final hold.

## Spatial Composition

The frame should feel like a dark room with a floor glow. Objects float in depth
but land in readable hero frames.

Good hero frames:

- one large prompt card with a CTA in the lower-right
- one large gradient CTA at center
- template cards orbiting or drifting around one large featured card
- a big local-app window clipped by the bottom edge
- a large circular provider node with labels around it
- a ring of model badges around a small center claim
- a folder or export object with pills bursting out

Bad hero frames:

- tiny UI cards pushed to one side
- flat web-page layout
- decorative background stronger than product objects
- long static card with only text changing

## Motion Grammar

- Text enters by typing or snapping into a half-typed state.
- Important nouns can smear horizontally before resolving.
- CTA clicks trigger sparkles, glow pulses, and scene changes.
- UI cards drift with parallax; one card becomes the hero.
- Integration/model badges move on arcs or rings.
- Export pills pop from a container, then drift outward.
- Major transitions use speed blur, white velocity wipes, or scale rushes.

Motion should feel physical and fast, but each hero frame must be readable.
