# Scene Blueprints

Use these as modules. Select only the modules that fit the product.

## 1. Kinetic Promise

Purpose: establish the product's promise or pain point.

Pattern:

- black stage, no UI yet
- large white phrase appears mid-typing
- phrase completes
- key nouns replace each other with horizontal blur
- optional logo lockup appears
- velocity wipe into next scene

Good copy:

- "Polished AI workflows"
- "Local-first automations"
- "Ship product demos"
- "Open source analytics"

## 2. Prompt Invocation

Purpose: show the product turning intent into output.

Pattern:

- tilted prompt card appears large in the left/center
- short prompt types in
- gradient CTA glows
- cursor clicks CTA
- sparkles and button pulse trigger transition

## 3. Generated Result Field

Purpose: make the product output feel abundant.

Pattern:

- multiple UI cards/templates float in black space
- one large featured card crosses the frame
- cards drift with parallax
- the cluster resolves into a single app canvas or board

## 4. Local Or Platform Capability

Purpose: communicate "runs locally", "works everywhere", or platform support.

Pattern:

- app window rises from bottom
- title types above the window
- small platform pills appear under title
- camera pushes in until title and pills are large

## 5. Connect Ecosystem

Purpose: show providers, apps, integrations, or data sources.

Pattern:

- large circular node enters along a curved slot
- labels or provider pills reveal around it
- node rotates or slides as more providers appear
- keep the node large; do not shrink to a remote cluster

## 6. Model Or Capability Ring

Purpose: show many models, agents, plugins, or capabilities.

Pattern:

- circular badge ring appears
- badges rotate or drift on orbital paths
- center text appears after the ring is readable
- badges may swap depth with scale and blur

## 7. Export Burst

Purpose: show output formats, delivery, or finished artifacts.

Pattern:

- headline appears with gradient export CTA
- cursor clicks CTA
- text smears away
- folder/container appears
- pills/cards burst outward: PDF, PPTX, Markdown, Video, API, Link

## 8. Final Claim

Purpose: end with a memorable product category or claim.

Pattern:

- large word appears alone
- second word joins with wide spacing
- phrase transforms through 2-4 states
- final phrase holds for at least 1 second

Examples:

- "Open" -> "Open source" -> "Open source AI" -> "AI workflow engine"
- "Prompt" -> "Prompt to product" -> "Product demo engine"
- "Local" -> "Local first" -> "Local AI workspace"
